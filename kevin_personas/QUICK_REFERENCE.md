# Investor Persona Quick Reference

## At a Glance

| Investor | Style | Primary Lens | Time Horizon | Risk Appetite | When to Use |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Warren Buffett | Quality value | Moat + owner earnings DCF | Decades | Low | Established businesses with strong moats |
| Charlie Munger | Quality value | ROIC + predictability | Decades | Low | Verifying a Buffett idea with extra rigor |
| Benjamin Graham | Deep value | Net-net / Graham Number | Months–years | Low | Beaten-down small/mid-caps below liquidation value |
| Michael Burry | Contrarian deep value | FCF yield + EV/EBIT | 1-3 years | Medium | Hated stocks with solid fundamentals |
| Peter Lynch | GARP | PEG ratio + ten-bagger | 3-5 years | Medium | Mid-cap growth at reasonable price |
| Phil Fisher | Quality growth | Revenue/EPS CAGR + R&D | 5-10 years | Medium | Compounders with strong management |
| Cathie Wood | Disruptive growth | TAM + R&D intensity | 5+ years | High | Emerging technology platforms |
| Stanley Druckenmiller | Momentum + asymmetry | Growth acceleration + price | 6-24 months | High | Strong momentum with clear risk/reward setup |
| Bill Ackman | Activist value | FCF + brand + activism | 2-5 years | Medium | Quality brands with operational improvement potential |
| Aswath Damodaran | Valuation | FCFF DCF + CAPM | Any | Any | Rigorous intrinsic value sanity check |
| Mohnish Pabrai | Downside-first value | FCF yield + net cash | 2-3 years | Low | Asymmetric bets with clear doubling path |
| Nassim Taleb | Antifragility | Tail risk + convexity | Any | Low (risk-focused) | Risk assessment on any position |
| Rakesh Jhunjhunwala | Quality growth value | ROE + CAGR + MOS | 5-10 years | Medium | High-quality growth businesses at a real discount |

## Primary Metrics by Persona

| Investor | #1 Signal | #2 Signal | #3 Signal | Hard Pass Triggers |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Buffett | Margin of safety vs intrinsic value | Consistent ROE >15% | Durable moat | Outside circle of competence |
| Munger | ROIC >15% consistently | FCF/NI ratio >1.1 | Business predictability | Complex business model |
| Graham | NCAV > Market Cap | Graham Number | Current ratio ≥2 | Price above Graham Number |
| Burry | FCF yield ≥8%+ | EV/EBIT <10 | Net cash position | High leverage with low FCF |
| Lynch | PEG ratio <1 | EPS growth >25% | Low D/E | Complicated/ununderstandable business |
| Fisher | Revenue/EPS CAGR >20% | R&D 3-15% of revenue | Stable margins | <3% R&D in tech-intensive market |
| Wood | Revenue growth acceleration | R&D >15% | Gross margin expansion | Decelerating growth or low R&D |
| Druckenmiller | Revenue/EPS CAGR + momentum | Asymmetric upside vs downside | Sentiment tailwind | Decelerating growth with high price |
| Ackman | Margin of safety vs DCF | Operating margin >15% | ROE >15% | Commodity business, no pricing power |
| Damodaran | Margin of safety vs FCFF DCF | Revenue CAGR >8% | ROIC >10% | No story connecting numbers to value |
| Pabrai | Net cash position | FCF yield >7%+ | Capex <5% of revenue | Net debt with low FCF |
| Taleb | Net cash / antifragility | D/E <0.3 | Stable margins CV <0.15 | High leverage, volatile earnings |
| Jhunjhunwala | Margin of safety ≥30% | ROE >20% | Revenue CAGR >20% | Declining margins, high debt |

## By Situation

### "This stock looks cheap but everyone hates it"
→ **Michael Burry** (is the hatred justified by fundamentals? Or contrarian opportunity?)  
→ **Ben Graham** (does it trade below Graham Number or NCAV?)  
→ **Mohnish Pabrai** (what's the downside protection? Can I see the doubling?)

### "This stock has been going up strongly"
→ **Stanley Druckenmiller** (is the momentum backed by fundamental growth acceleration?)  
→ **Phil Fisher** (is the growth sustainable for 5+ more years?)  
→ **Aswath Damodaran** (does the momentum-driven price still have a margin of safety?)

### "This is a technology/AI company"
→ **Cathie Wood** (is this genuinely disruptive or just incremental?)  
→ **Nassim Taleb** (what's the tail risk? Is there R&D optionality/convexity?)  
→ **Charlie Munger** (is the moat real and is the business predictable enough?)

### "I want to stress-test my thesis before buying"
→ **Nassim Taleb** (what are the fragility risks and tail events?)  
→ **Aswath Damodaran** (what assumptions does the current price require?)  
→ **Ben Graham** (what is the hard asset value floor if things go wrong?)

### "I already own this and wondering whether to hold"
→ **Warren Buffett** (has the moat weakened? Is intrinsic value still growing?)  
→ **Phil Fisher** (is R&D investment sustaining the innovation pipeline?)  
→ **Mohnish Pabrai** (is the path to doubling still intact?)

### "This is a quality company but feels expensive"
→ **Peter Lynch** (check the PEG — what's the growth rate justifying the P/E?)  
→ **Charlie Munger** (would you pay a fair price for a wonderful business?)  
→ **Aswath Damodaran** (run the DCF — what growth rate does the price imply?)

## Valuations Each Persona Runs

| Persona | Primary Valuation | Key Assumptions |
|---|---|---|
| Buffett | 3-stage owner earnings DCF | 10% discount, Stage 1 ≤8%, Terminal 2.5%, 15% haircut |
| Munger | FCF × 10/15/20 multiples | Normalize FCF over 3-5 years |
| Graham | Graham Number + NCAV | √(22.5 × EPS × BVPS); Current Assets − Total Liabilities |
| Burry | FCF yield + EV/EBIT | No intrinsic value model; yield vs cost of capital |
| Lynch | PEG ratio | P/E ÷ (EPS CAGR × 100) |
| Fisher | P/E <20, P/FCF <20 | Willing to pay premium for quality |
| Wood | DCF: 20% growth, 15% discount, 25× terminal | Very aggressive growth assumptions |
| Druckenmiller | P/E, P/FCF, EV/EBIT, EV/EBITDA | Willing to pay up for growth |
| Ackman | DCF: 6% growth, 10% discount, 15× terminal | Conservative; requires >30% MOS |
| Damodaran | FCFF DCF via CAPM | Revenue CAGR → growth, risk-free 4% + Beta × 5% ERP |
| Pabrai | FCF × 10-13 (normalized) | Downside protection > upside; requires net cash |
| Taleb | No DCF — remove fragile first | Focus on antifragility, not return |
| Jhunjhunwala | DCF: CAGR-based, quality-tiered discount | 12-18% discount; 12-18× terminal; ≥30% MOS required |
